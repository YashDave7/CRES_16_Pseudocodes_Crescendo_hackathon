Why do we need EHR?

EHRs are digital versions of medical records with patient information. They improve patient care, reduce errors, enhance communication, increase efficiency, and facilitate research. They're required by law in some healthcare organizations. EHRs also improve patient engagement by providing them access to their records and test results.
